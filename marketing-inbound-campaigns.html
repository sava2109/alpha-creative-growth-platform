About workflows

Inbound campaigns start by attracting visitors through SEO-optimized landing pages, blogs, social media, and Google Ads. Lead magnets like eBooks, webinars, and free trials capture visitor information and nurture them through the sales funnel.
Engagement is maintained through email nurturing workflows with automated sequences, personalized content, and targeted offers. A/B testing refines messaging and conversions.

Continuous monitoring of key metrics, such as traffic and lead conversion rates, ensures strategies are adjusted for optimal performance, driving sustainable lead generation and stronger customer relationships. Therefore, we are here to support you in:
Attracting and converting more leads through SEO and Google Ads
Nurture leads with the help of email marketing, blogs and social media
Automate customer communication and manage relationships

Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can find answers to our most frequently asked questions. If you have additional questions we haven’t covered, please reach out to us at hello@alphacretive.agency

How will the marketing workflows be set up?

We’ll implement automated workflows using HubSpot or other platforms, depending on your needs. These workflows will include automated email or newsletter campaigns, lead nurturing sequences, and customer journey touchpoints that engage prospects at every stage of the funnel.

Do you offer Google Ads setup and management?

Yes! We’ll set up Google Ads campaigns for your inbound campaign, including ad copy creation, targeting, and budget management. We’ll also ensure that your ads align with your landing pages and content strategy to drive high-quality traffic.

Will you handle the SEO for the landing pages?

Yes! We’ll optimize desired landing pages for SEO to ensure they rank well in search engines, helping drive organic traffic to your site. We focus on keyword optimization, content structure, and technical SEO elements to maximize visibility. Have in mind that SEO is long term strategy and it will take some time to generate results.

What makes your inbound campaign different from others?

We specialize in real estate, architecture, and interior design, combining industry-specific expertise with HubSpot-certified skills to create not just lead generation but an effective customer journey. While these are our primary focus areas, we also work with businesses across other industries.
