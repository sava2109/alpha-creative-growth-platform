Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can find answers to our most frequently asked questions. If you have additional questions we haven’t covered, please reach out to us at hello@alphacretive.agency

How long does it take to develop an e-commerce website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project and inventory, but generally, it takes around 6–10 weeks to complete an e-commerce website, including design, development, and testing.

Can you integrate payment gateways into my online store?

Yes, we can integrate secure payment gateways such as PayPal, Stripe, and others to ensure smooth transactions for your customers.

What e-commerce platforms do you work with?

Depending on your needs, we either work with leading e-commerce platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and BigCommerce, or create a custom-built solution tailored to your business.

Do you offer ongoing support after launching the e-commerce site?

Absolutely! We offer maintenance and support packages, as well as inventory management to ensure your online store remains secure, updated, and fully functional post-launch.
